Tuesday March 7th 2017

12:47

A 28yr old male begins his journey with a long rectangular ticket in his pocket marked with a four digit number, a date, and a name.

12:50

Traveling north he stops in a falafel shop. Regular with couscous, cucumber, pickled eggplant, zhug and tahini.

13:12

He exchanges his ticket for a thick green and white envelope and looks toward the south.

13:25

She appears to him among the crowd. Kneeling, shrouded in black and gazing through the floor. He passes by once, then returns. Approaching silently he places his offering into the tattered ceremonial cup in front of her.

13:32

Purification, his journey ends where it began.
